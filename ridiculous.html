The Fight that Ended it All

One day, two boys, two grades above me, got into a fight over their tamagotchi toys. A few minutes later, the verbal argument became something bigger: The one boy broke the other boy's toy. Because of their verbal argument and the destruction of a personal item, every grade was prohibited to bring in any toys from home. We were stuck indoors either being forced to draw or play 7 up. Being stuck in the classroom during playtime was no longer fun and felt forced.
